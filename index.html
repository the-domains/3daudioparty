Thursday, September 1st @ 11:45am - Fifth Avenue Room

Through the Looking Glass: 3D Audio & Your VR Game’s World

Presenters: Joshua Du Chene, Jacob Pernell, Gordon McGladdery, Carlye Nyte

Ambisonics, surround sound, binaural, spatialization, HRTF… Audio for virtual reality can be confusing, and as developers and audio professionals in this modern VR era, it’s critical to understand what these things are/aren’t and how they’ll make or break your game. Join the audio geeks behind Fantastic Contraption, Fun Bits, AudioVR.com, & Tomorrow Today Labs VR as they demystify and clarify 3D sound and provide you the tools & info you need to create immersive audio experiences for your players.
